the meridian body

Acupuncture points and meridian channels, located on your own hand in real time by proportional measurement (cun), not fixed pixels. Turn your hand over to move between the palmar and dorsal channels; explore the points with your other index fingertip, and trace a channel end to end to open it. Open all six. Locations follow the WHO standard; this is a design source and cultural coordinate system, not medical advice. On-device, nothing stored.
